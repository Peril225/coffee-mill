Our History

WHEN COFFEE MILL WAS FOUNDED IN 1974 WE OFFERED 2 REGULAR COFFEE BLENDS AND ONE DECAF. TODAY, COFFEE MILL OFFERS MORE THAN 30 DIFFERENT COFFEE BLENDS FROM AROUND THE WORLD.

Though the coffee offerings have changed considerably the commitment to service remains as strong. Coffee Mill doesn’t simply drop
supplies on your doorstep, we are a service-oriented organization. With each visit our staff cleans and exchanges your airpots and carafes,
test your office coffee machine equipment, and cleans and organizes the beverage area.

At Coffee Mill, our staff takes great pride in their work, and it shows in terms of their longevity. The average tenure among the approx
one dozen Coffee Mill drivers is a remarkable 10-plus years. Recently Coffee Mill celebrated one employee’s 37th anniversary
with the business! We are a leader in office coffee machines in the Twin Cities MN.

Over the past four decades Coffee Mill has built a stellar reputation in the industry, as not necessarily the biggest, but the best. In 2011 the
company was honored to become Dunn Bros. exclusive office coffee supplier, a true testament to the company’s focus on outstanding
customer service.
